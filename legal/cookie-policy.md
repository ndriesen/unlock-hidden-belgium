This Cookie Policy explains how Unlock Hidden Belgium ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you access our website or application (the "Service").

This policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website or use an application.

Cookies help websites function properly and provide information about how users interact with the platform.

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the operation of the Service.

They enable core functionality such as:

- user authentication
- security
- session management
- platform navigation.

Without these cookies, certain features of the Service may not function properly.

### Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics tools to understand how users interact with the Service.

These cookies may collect information such as:

- pages visited
- time spent on pages
- device type
- approximate location.

This information helps improve the performance and usability of the platform.

### Functional Cookies

Functional cookies allow the platform to remember certain preferences, such as:

- language settings
- login sessions
- user interface preferences.

## 3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be placed by third-party services integrated into the platform.

Examples may include:

- analytics providers
- infrastructure services
- embedded content providers.

Unlock Hidden Belgium does not control these third-party cookies.

Users should consult the privacy policies of those third parties for additional information.

## 4. Managing Cookies

Users can manage or disable cookies through their browser settings.

Most browsers allow users to:

- delete cookies
- block cookies
- receive notifications when cookies are used.

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain parts of the Service.

## 5. Consent

Where required by applicable law, we will request your consent before placing non-essential cookies on your device.

Users may withdraw consent at any time through cookie preference settings where available.

## 6. Changes to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically.

Updated versions will be posted on the platform with a revised "Last updated" date.
