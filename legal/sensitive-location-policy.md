# Sensitive Location Policy

Last updated: [DATE]

Unlock Hidden Belgium is designed to help users discover unique and lesser-known places.

However, some locations may require additional protection due to environmental, safety, or privacy concerns. This policy explains how we manage such locations.

## 1. Purpose of this Policy

Certain places may be sensitive because they involve:

- protected natural areas
- fragile ecosystems
- private property
- cultural heritage sites
- locations with safety risks.

This policy allows Unlock Hidden Belgium to limit or remove content that may negatively impact such locations.

## 2. Environmentally Sensitive Areas

Some natural environments can be damaged by excessive visitation.

Examples may include:

- protected wildlife habitats
- fragile landscapes
- conservation areas
- restricted natural reserves.

If a location is determined to be environmentally sensitive, Unlock Hidden Belgium may:

- remove the location from the platform
- hide the exact coordinates
- limit the visibility of the location.

## 3. Private Property

Locations located on or near private property may be restricted or removed if:

- the property owner requests removal
- access to the location is not legally permitted
- the listing encourages trespassing.

Unlock Hidden Belgium respects property rights and may remove such listings at its discretion.

## 4. Safety Concerns

Some locations may pose safety risks due to:

- dangerous terrain
- unstable structures
- hazardous environments
- restricted or prohibited access areas.

If a location is determined to present safety risks, we may restrict or remove the listing.

## 5. Cultural and Historical Protection

Certain locations may require protection due to cultural, archaeological, or historical significance.

Content that risks damaging or exposing such sites may be removed or restricted.

## 6. Requests for Location Removal

Property owners, authorities, or concerned individuals may request the removal of a location listing.

Requests should include:

- identification of the location
- explanation of the concern
- supporting documentation if applicable.

## 7. Platform Discretion

Unlock Hidden Belgium reserves the right to:

- remove or modify location listings
- hide specific location details
- restrict visibility of sensitive locations
- remove associated content such as photos or descriptions.

These actions may be taken without prior notice if necessary to protect safety, privacy, or environmental integrity.

## 8. Responsible Exploration

Users are encouraged to explore responsibly and respect:

- local regulations
- private property
- environmental protection rules.

Users remain solely responsible for their actions when visiting locations discovered through the platform.

## 9. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically.

Updated versions will be published on the platform with a revised "Last updated" date.
