# Content Removal Policy

Last updated: [DATE]

Unlock Hidden Belgium provides a platform where users may share locations, photos, and travel recommendations.

This policy explains how individuals or organizations may request the removal of content.

## 1. Types of Removal Requests

Content removal requests may relate to:

- copyright infringement
- privacy concerns
- incorrect or misleading information
- unauthorized photographs
- safety concerns regarding location disclosure.

## 2. Submitting a Removal Request

To request removal of content, please provide:

- a description of the content
- the URL or location of the content on the platform
- the reason for the removal request
- supporting documentation if applicable
- your contact information.

Requests may be submitted to:

[EMAIL]

## 3. Review Process

Upon receiving a request, Unlock Hidden Belgium may:

- review the content
- request additional information
- temporarily restrict access to the content
- remove the content if appropriate.

We reserve the right to determine whether content violates our policies.

## 4. Discretion of the Platform

Unlock Hidden Belgium may remove or restrict content at its sole discretion, including when content may pose:

- legal risks
- privacy risks
- safety concerns.

## 5. No Guarantee of Removal

Submitting a removal request does not guarantee that content will be removed.

Each request is evaluated on a case-by-case basis.
