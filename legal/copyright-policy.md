# Copyright Policy

Last updated: [DATE]

Unlock Hidden Belgium respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects users of the platform to do the same.

This policy describes how copyright infringement claims can be reported and addressed.

## 1. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring that any content they upload to the platform does not infringe the copyright, trademark, or other intellectual property rights of third parties.

Content may include:

- photographs
- written descriptions
- reviews
- location submissions
- other media.

## 2. Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you believe that content on the platform infringes your copyright, you may submit a copyright infringement notice.

The notice must include:

- identification of the copyrighted work
- identification of the allegedly infringing material
- sufficient information to locate the material on the platform
- your contact information
- a statement that you have a good faith belief the use is unauthorized
- a statement that the information provided is accurate.

## 3. Content Removal

Upon receiving a valid copyright complaint, Unlock Hidden Belgium may:

- remove the allegedly infringing content
- restrict access to the content
- notify the user who submitted the content.

## 4. Repeat Infringers

Users who repeatedly upload infringing content may have their accounts suspended or permanently terminated.

## 5. Limitation of Responsibility

Unlock Hidden Belgium does not actively monitor all content uploaded by users.

The platform acts as an intermediary and removes content when properly notified of alleged infringement.
